01 — About Me

Maker.
Thinker. Doer.

I have never been someone who stays in one lane. I have worked property maintenance and excelled at it. I built a full workshop from the concrete slab to the roof with my father, and people walk in and genuinely stop talking mid-sentence. I hold a Data and BI role at a company I am proud of, designing data infrastructure that did not exist before I arrived.

The throughline is not the industry. It is the mindset. As a kid I was the one sitting on the toilet explaining my latest invention to anyone who would listen. One family classic: a hat that brushed your teeth, fixed your hair, and cleaned your ears. This was before I had ever seen The Jetsons. Some things do not change.

My most recent project is a hand-tufted rug that now hangs in my living room. I found an artist whose work I loved, decided the price tag was not happening, bought the gear, built the frame out of 2x4s, designed the pattern in Adobe, and went to work. Everyone who walks in asks about it. I built it, and I get to say that. That feeling is what drives everything I do, whether it is a Power BI data pipeline or a stained glass mirror I have not started yet.
